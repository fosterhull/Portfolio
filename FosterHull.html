1. Hippo Campus Concert

My Role:

  • Collaborate with team members and student body to plan and execute exciting concert programming
  • Communicate closely with our agent and artist to facilitate a smooth event process
  • Develop designs for newsletters and posters, as well as employ other necessary advertising strategies to increase student body attendance

Our Success:

  • While in my position as a co-chair of the concerts committee at Luther College we were struggling to get turn-out at concerts. We had about 450 students at the concert when I was a sophomore, out of a student body of about 2,000. I began working in the position at the end of my sophomore year and throughout the year our team collaborated heavily on restructuring the format of choosing artists. We created a bracket system in which the student body chose the artist for the following fall semester. By the time of the fall concert of 2024 the student body had shrunk to just shy of 1,600. The attendance for that concert was over 700. Obviously there are further details that contributed to that outcome, however my passion and collaboration with other members of the Student Activities Council to re-establish Luther concerts among the student body was very evident throughout this process and it paid off.

5. Graphic Design II Project: The Airline Ticket

Project Description:

  • The paper boarding pass is very much a utilitarian object. It has existed for one purpose: tell you where you are going and when. But just because it has a narrow function does not mean it needs to suffer from poor design.
  • This is where you come in. Taking into account all the information that needs to be addressed in the boarding pass, what information may (if any) be absent from the pass that could be useful, and the different potential audiences the pass will have, you are charged with the challenge of redesigning this slip of paper to improve both its function and its form.

Requirement:

  • Airline branding
  • Passenger Name
  • Departure City
  • Destination City
  • Boarding Time
  • Flight Number
  • Boarding Group
  • Terminal
  • Gate Number
  • Seat Number
  • Barcode
